Teacher Protest in Nepal

May 7, 2025

Teachers in Nepal have recently launched a nationwide protest to demand better working conditions, higher salaries, and job security. The protest comes after years of unmet promises from the government regarding education sector reforms.

Why the Protest?

The teachers are demanding better pay, equal opportunities, and improved working environments. The protest has gained significant attention, with teachers marching across the streets of major cities in Nepal.

What is the Government's Response?

The government has promised to address the teachers' demands in the coming months, but there is growing uncertainty among teachers regarding the effectiveness of these promises.
